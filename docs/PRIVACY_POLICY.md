# Tarteb Privacy Policy (Draft)

**Last updated:** June 2026  
**Contact:** privacy@tarteb.app

Tarteb (“we”, “the app”) is a job marketplace for the UAE. This draft is for App Store / Play Store review; publish the same text on your website before launch.

## What we collect

- **Phone number** — sign-in (OTP), employer/candidate profiles, and contact unlock flows.
- **Profile data** — name, role, location, visa status, salary expectations, photo (candidates), company details (employers).
- **Usage** — app interactions needed to operate browse, unlock, and subscription features.
- **Push token** — if you enable notifications, to send alerts (profile views, unlocks, subscription status).
- **Device** — standard mobile diagnostics when crash reporting is enabled (production only).

## How we use data

- Authenticate users and match employers with candidates.
- Send SMS OTP via our messaging provider (Twilio).
- Send push notifications you opt into.
- Operate subscriptions and employer unlock limits.
- Improve reliability and security.

## Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is processed on **Supabase** (hosting/database) and messaging/payment partners required to run the service (e.g. Twilio, WhatsApp links for subscription support).

## Retention

Account data is kept while your account is active. You may request deletion via in-app settings or privacy@tarteb.app.

## Your choices

- Deny notification permission — the app still works; you will not receive push alerts.
- Sign out — clears the local session; contact us to delete server-side profile data.

## Children

Tarteb is not directed at users under 18.

## Changes

We will post updates in the app and on tarteb.app/privacy.

## UAE users

Processing is based on providing the service you request and legitimate business interests in operating a UAE-focused marketplace.
